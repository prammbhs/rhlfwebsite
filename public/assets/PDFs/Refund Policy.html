Refund Policy
 Real Happiness Of Life Foundation has instituted a donation refund policy to ensure fair
and transparent processing of requests for refund of donations as digital payments are
becoming more frequent.
 Real Happiness Of Life Foundation expects that all donors will exercise due care and
diligence while making donations.
 Real Happiness Of Life Foundation also recognizes that a donation may be made
erroneously or donors may change their mind.
 Real Happiness Of Life Foundation will examine request for refund of donation and
endeavour to make the refund.
 Real Happiness Of Life Foundation may also seek proper explanation and reason from
the user.
 Also, it will require further verification of the user and need documents of proof as well
as for donation.
 Real Happiness Of Life Foundation is not obliged to make refunds and may, in its
discretion, decline any requests for refund of donations, particularly if a tax exemption
certificate has been issued.

information from third parties such as Payment Gateway, Banks, etc. through which
